Travel Tips

Packing Tips

Pack light! Most places have laundry services or facilities. Be sure to bring comfortable shoes for walking and a light jacket for cooler evenings.

Safety Tips

Keep your valuables in a secure place and be aware of your surroundings. It's always good to have a copy of your important documents.

Cultural Tips

Learn a few basic phrases in the local language. The locals appreciate the effort and it can enhance your travel experience.
